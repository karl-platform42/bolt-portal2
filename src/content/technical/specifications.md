# Technical Specifications

## System Requirements

- Power Supply: 380-480V AC, 50/60 Hz
- Operating Temperature: 0°C to 40°C
- Humidity Range: 20% to 80% (non-condensing)
- Protection Class: IP54
- Network Interface: Ethernet (TCP/IP)
